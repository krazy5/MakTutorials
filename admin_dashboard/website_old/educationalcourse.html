What is MHT CET exam?

MHT CET or Maharashtra Common Entrance Test, is a state-level entrance test conducted by the Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell for candidates to take admission in the engineering, pharmacy and technology programs offered by private as well as government colleges in Maharashtra. Check MHT CET Colleges

MHT CET 2024 Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must meet the following eligibility criteria to apply for the MHT CET 2024 exam:

  • Nationality: The candidate must be an Indian citizen.
  • Age: There is no age limit for appearing in MHT CET 2024.
  • Educational Qualification: The candidate should have passed the 10+2 or equivalent exam from a recognised board.
  • Subjects:
    • For Engineering and Technology courses: The candidate must have passed 10+2 or equivalent exams in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
    • For Pharmacy and Pharm. D courses: The candidate must have passed 10+2 or equivalent exam with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology.
  • Minimum Marks: The candidate must have secured a minimum of 50% marks (45% for reserved category candidates) in the qualifying exam.
  • Domicile: The candidate must be a domicile of Maharashtra.

The MHT CET eligibility criteria may vary depending on the course and institute. Candidates are advised to check the eligibility criteria of their desired course/institute before applying for the MHT CET 2024.


MHT CET 2024 Registration

The registration process for MHT CET 2024 is expected to start tentatively in March 2024. The MHT CET 2024 registration process can be completed online through the official website of the Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell.

Here are the steps involved in the MHT CET 2024 registration process:

  • Visit the official website of Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell (https://cetcell.mahacet.org/).
  • Click on the "MHT CET 2024 Registration" link.
  • Fill in the required details, such as personal, academic, and contact information.
  • Upload your photograph and signature in the prescribed format and size.
  • Pay the application fee through the available online payment modes.
  • Verify and submit the MHT CET 2024 application form.
  • Take a printout of the application form and fee receipt for future reference.

It's important to note that the exact registration process may be subject to change, so it's recommended to check the official website of Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test Cell for the latest updates and instructions regarding the registration process.

MHT CET Admit Card

MHT CET 2024 Admit Card

The MHT CET 2024 admit card will be available on the official website of the State Common Entrance Test Cell, Maharashtra, tentatively in May 2024. Candidates can download their admit card by logging in to their account using their application number and password. The admit card will contain important information such as the candidate's name, roll number, exam date and time, exam centre details, and important instructions. The steps to download the MHT CET 2024 admit card is as follows:

  • Visit the official website of MHT CET 2024
  • Click on the "Download Admit Card" link.
  • Enter your Application Number and Password/Date of Birth and then click on the "Submit" button.
  • The MHT CET 2024 admit card will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of the admit card for future reference.

It is important to note that the MHT CET 2024 admit card is a mandatory document to be carried to the exam centre, without which candidates will not be allowed to appear for the MHT CET 2024 exam.

MHT CET 2024 Results

The MHT CET 2024 Results are expected to be declared in June or July 2024 tentatively. The results will be available on the official website of MHT CET. Candidates can check their results by entering their application number and date of birth. The results will be in the form of a scorecard and will contain details such as the candidate's name, roll number, total marks scored, section-wise marks, and percentile rank. Candidates who qualify for the MHT CET 2024 will be eligible for admission to various undergraduate Engineering, Pharmacy, and Agricultural courses offered by colleges in Maharashtra.

The MHT CET 2024 results can be downloaded by following the steps below:

  • Visit the official website of MHT CET 2024
  • Click on the link for MHT CET 2024 Results.
  • Enter your roll number and other required details.
  • Click on the Submit button.
  • The MHT CET 2024 Result will be displayed on the screen.
  • Download and take a printout of the MHT CET 2024 Result for future reference.
